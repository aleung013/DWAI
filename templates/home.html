Hello, world!

Google Paint is a site where multiple people can work on a single work of art at the same time.

A quick and easy online tool!

Users have access to our canvas, which includes basic functionality such as drawing, colors, pen sizes, undos and redos. All finished works can be saved in their personal galleries, to be accessed at a click of their mouse.

Instantaneous Collaboration

Users can draw with their friends by simultaneously working on the same canvas.
